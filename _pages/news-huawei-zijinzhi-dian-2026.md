In July 2026, Xinlai Kang, Dunyao Xue, and Junyi Lin were invited to Huawei's Huang Danian Chasiwu for the "Zijin Summit" Young Scholars Paper Sharing Seminar, where they presented and discussed their recent research.

The seminar offered a chance for direct exchange with industry researchers. The discussions covered methodological design, practical problem formulation, and possible follow-up applications, further strengthening the group's connection with real industrial questions.
