About BDAL-RUC
======
<p style="text-align: justify;">
BDAL-RUC is led by Cheng Meng, a tenure-track Assistant Professor and Ph.D. advisor at the School of Statistics and Data Science, Renmin University of China. Our research focuses on Big Data Statistics, Optimal Transport, Artificial Intelligence, and Industrial Statistics, with an emphasis on theory-backed scalable methods for real-world applications.
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">
The group has received a Junior Researcher Award from the Pan-Asian Committee for Statistical Research, three Huawei Spark Awards, one Wu Yuzhang Honorary Scholarship, two JSM Student Paper Awards, and multiple National Graduate Scholarships. We aim to build reliable statistical tools that can move from theory to large-scale industrial and scientific practice.
</p>
